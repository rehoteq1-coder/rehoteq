Privacy Policy

How Rehoteq Technologies collects, uses and protects your information

At Rehoteq Technologies, we take your privacy seriously. This policy explains what information we collect when you use our website, products and services, and how we use it. By using rehoteq.com, you agree to the terms of this policy.

1. Who We Are

Rehoteq Technologies is a Nigerian technology company based in Okitipupa, Ondo State, Nigeria. We provide cloud-based school result management systems, cybersecurity tools (RehoCheck), solar energy installation, electrical engineering services and computer hardware services.

Our website is located at rehoteq.com and our contact email is [email protected].

2. Information We Collect

We collect information in the following ways:

  • Contact information — When you fill our service request form or contact us via WhatsApp, we collect your name, phone number and service request details.
  • School data — When schools subscribe to our RMS system, we collect school name, administrator contact details and student result data as provided by the school.
  • Advertising submissions — When you submit an ad through our news portal, we collect your school name, ad details and contact information.
  • Usage data — We may collect non-personal information about how visitors use our website, such as pages visited and browser type, through Google Analytics.
  • RehoCheck data — When you scan a URL using RehoCheck, the URL is processed through third-party security services (Google Safe Browsing and VirusTotal). We do not store scanned URLs permanently.

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Provide and improve our services to you
  • Process service requests and respond to enquiries
  • Manage school subscriptions and result management accounts
  • Publish approved school advertisements on our news portal
  • Send important updates about your subscription or service
  • Improve our website and user experience
  • Comply with legal obligations

We do not sell, trade or rent your personal information to any third party for marketing purposes.

4. Google AdSense and Advertising

Our website uses Google AdSense to display advertisements. Google may use cookies to show ads based on your previous visits to our website and other websites on the internet. You can opt out of personalised advertising by visiting Google Ad Settings.

Third-party vendors, including Google, use cookies to serve ads based on your prior visits to our website. The use of advertising cookies enables Google and its partners to serve ads to you based on your visit to our site and other sites on the internet.

5. Cookies

Our website uses cookies to enhance your browsing experience. Cookies are small text files stored on your device that help us understand how you use our site. We use:

  • Essential cookies — Necessary for the website to function properly
  • Analytics cookies — Help us understand how visitors interact with our website (Google Analytics)
  • Advertising cookies — Used by Google AdSense to serve relevant advertisements

You can control cookies through your browser settings. Disabling cookies may affect some features of our website.

6. Data Security

We take the security of your data seriously. We implement appropriate technical and organisational measures to protect your personal information against unauthorised access, alteration, disclosure or destruction.

School result data stored in our RMS system is protected by Firebase security rules that restrict access to authorised users only. Student result links can be deactivated by school administrators at any time.

However, no method of internet transmission or electronic storage is 100 percent secure. While we strive to use commercially acceptable means to protect your personal information, we cannot guarantee its absolute security.

7. Third-Party Services

Our services integrate with the following third-party platforms:

  • Google Firebase — Used for storing school result data. Subject to Google Privacy Policy.
  • Google Safe Browsing — Used in RehoCheck for URL safety verification.
  • VirusTotal — Used in RehoCheck for malware scanning.
  • Groq AI — Used in RehoCheck for AI-powered analysis.
  • Google AdSense — Used for displaying advertisements on our website.
  • Paystack — Used for processing card payments. Subject to Paystack Privacy Policy.

Each of these services has their own privacy policies governing the use of your information.

8. Children's Privacy

Our RMS system processes student data on behalf of schools. Schools are responsible for obtaining appropriate consent from parents or guardians before providing student information to our system. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13 years of age without parental consent obtained through the school.

9. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Request access to the personal information we hold about you
  • Request correction of inaccurate information
  • Request deletion of your personal data
  • Withdraw consent for data processing at any time
  • Lodge a complaint with the relevant data protection authority

To exercise any of these rights, please contact us using the details below.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect changes in our practices or for other operational, legal or regulatory reasons. We will notify you of any significant changes by posting the new policy on this page with an updated date.

We encourage you to review this policy periodically to stay informed about how we protect your information.
