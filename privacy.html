2. Information We Collect

We collect different types of information depending on how you use our services:

2.1 Information You Provide Directly

  • Contact information — name, phone number, email address when you contact us via WhatsApp, email, or our contact form
  • School onboarding data — school name, address, contact person details, and plan selection when a school registers for Rehoteq RMS
  • Student and staff data in RMS — entered by school administrators on behalf of their institutions. This includes student names, registration numbers, class, scores, and parent contact details
  • Calculator inputs — appliance selections, room configurations, and budget settings entered into our free calculators. These are processed locally in your browser and are not stored on our servers unless you explicitly send them to us via the WhatsApp quote button

2.2 Information Collected Automatically

  • Usage data — pages visited, time spent, links clicked, browser type, device type, and operating system — collected via Google Analytics if enabled
  • IP address — your internet protocol address, used for approximate geographic location and security purposes
  • Cookies — small text files stored in your browser. See Section 4 for details

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect for the following purposes:

  • Providing our services — to deliver the Rehoteq RMS, process school registrations, and respond to service enquiries
  • Customer support — to respond to your questions, complaints, or requests via WhatsApp, phone, or email
  • Improving our products — to understand how our tools and calculators are used, identify problems, and build better features
  • Sending service communications — important updates about your account, system changes, or service disruptions. We do not send marketing emails without your consent
  • Legal compliance — to comply with applicable Nigerian laws and regulations
  • Security — to detect and prevent fraud, abuse, or unauthorised access
We do not sell your personal information to any third party. We do not share your data with advertisers for targeting purposes beyond what Google AdSense collects through its standard advertising technology (see Section 5).

4. Cookies and Tracking

Our website uses cookies — small data files stored in your browser — to improve your experience and understand how our site is used.

Types of cookies we use:

  • Essential cookies — required for the website to function. Cannot be disabled.
  • Analytics cookies — used by Google Analytics to measure traffic and understand user behaviour in aggregate. No personally identifying information is shared.
  • Advertising cookies — set by Google AdSense to serve relevant advertisements. These may use your browsing history across websites to personalise ads.

You can control cookies through your browser settings. Most browsers allow you to refuse cookies, delete existing cookies, or receive a warning before a cookie is set. Note that disabling cookies may affect some website functionality.

To opt out of Google's interest-based advertising, visit google.com/settings/ads or aboutads.info.

7. Data Storage and Security

Data entered into the Rehoteq RMS is stored on Google Firebase servers, which are located in data centres operated by Google. Firebase complies with major international data protection standards.

We implement reasonable technical and organisational measures to protect your personal information against unauthorised access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction. These measures include encrypted connections (HTTPS), access controls, and regular security reviews.

However, no method of internet transmission or electronic storage is 100% secure. While we strive to protect your data, we cannot guarantee absolute security.

Calculator inputs are processed entirely within your browser using JavaScript and are not transmitted to or stored on our servers unless you choose to send them via the WhatsApp quote button.

  • Right to rectification — to request correction of inaccurate data
  • Right to erasure — to request deletion of your personal data, where we have no legal obligation to retain it

For RMS data held on behalf of schools — as the data controller for their students and staff — requests should be directed to the relevant school administration.

9. Children's Privacy

The Rehoteq RMS processes student data on behalf of schools. This may include data about students who are under 18 years of age. This data is processed solely at the direction of the school administration and is used only for result management and academic reporting purposes. We do not use student data for advertising, profiling, or any commercial purpose.

Our public website and calculators are not directed at children under 13 years of age, and we do not knowingly collect personal information directly from children under 13.
