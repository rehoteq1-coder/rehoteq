Our Story
From Okitipupa to the World

Rehoteq Technologies was founded with a simple but powerful idea — that Nigerian schools, businesses and homes deserve access to world-class technology solutions at affordable prices.

We started by identifying the real problems our community faced every day. Schools spending weeks computing results by hand. Families losing money to WhatsApp scammers. Homes battling unreliable electricity. Computers breaking down with no expert nearby.

We built solutions for every one of these problems. Today, Rehoteq Technologies operates from Okitipupa, Ondo State, serving clients across the region with cloud-based software, cybersecurity tools, solar energy systems and computer hardware services.

Our name Rehoteq comes from a simple belief — Reliable, Honest Technology for everyone. That is our promise to every client we serve.

Founder and Chief Executive Officer
Adetoye is a certified electrical and solar PV engineer as well as a computer hardware engineer based in Okitipupa, Ondo State. With years of hands-on experience in electrical installations, solar energy systems and computer hardware repairs, he founded Rehoteq Technologies to bridge the technology gap in his community. His vision is to make quality digital tools accessible to Nigerian schools and businesses regardless of their size or location. Under his leadership, Rehoteq has developed two live applications — the School Result Management System and RehoCheck cybersecurity platform — serving students, parents and schools across Nigeria.
