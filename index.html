Quick Tech Tips

Stay Informed
& Protected.

Practical tips to keep your devices, data and money safe in Nigeria.

Always Verify Before You Click
Before clicking any link sent via WhatsApp or SMS, paste it into RehoCheck first. Phishing attacks are Nigeria's fastest growing cybercrime.
Solar Saves More Than You Think
A 2kW solar system can eliminate over 70% of your electricity bill. ROI in 2–3 years in Nigeria.
Clean Your PC Every 6 Months
Dust causes overheating. Regular cleaning extends computer life by 3–5 years and prevents costly repairs.
Strong Password = Strong Protection
Use 12+ characters mixing letters, numbers and symbols. Never reuse passwords across accounts.
Check Wiring Every 5 Years
Old or damaged wiring is the leading cause of electrical fires in Nigeria. A professional inspection can prevent disaster.
Digital Records Never Get Lost
Paper can burn, flood or fade. Cloud-based school result systems keep student records safe and accessible forever.
