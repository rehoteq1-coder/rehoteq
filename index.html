Tech News & Tips

Stay Informed & Protected

Latest technology news, cybersecurity alerts and practical tips — updated regularly to keep you ahead.

💡 Quick Tech Tips

🔐
Always Verify Before You Click
Before clicking any link sent via WhatsApp or SMS, paste it into RehoCheck first. Phishing attacks are Nigeria's fastest growing cybercrime.
☀️
Solar Saves More Than You Think
A properly sized 2kW solar system can eliminate over 70% of your electricity bill. The investment typically pays back within 2-3 years in Nigeria.
💻
Clean Your PC Every 6 Months
Dust buildup causes overheating and slow performance. Regular maintenance extends computer life by 3-5 years and prevents costly repairs.
📱
Strong Password = Strong Protection
Use at least 12 characters mixing letters, numbers and symbols. Never use the same password for multiple accounts. Consider a password manager.
⚡
Check Wiring Every 5 Years
Old or damaged wiring is the leading cause of electrical fires in Nigeria. A professional inspection can prevent disaster and save lives.
🎓
Digital Records Never Get Lost
Paper records can burn, flood or fade. Cloud-based school result systems keep student records safe forever — accessible from anywhere.
