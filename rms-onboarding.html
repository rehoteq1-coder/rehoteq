Memorandum of Understanding (MOU)

This Memorandum of Understanding ("Agreement") is entered into between Rehoteq Technologies, a technology company registered and operating in Okitipupa, Ondo State, Nigeria ("Service Provider"), and the undersigned school institution ("Client School").

1. Nature of Service

Rehoteq Technologies agrees to provide the Client School access to the Rehoteq Result Management System (RMS) — a cloud-based school result management platform. The service includes student result entry, auto-calculation of scores and GPA, cumulative 3-term tracking, student result access links, and CSV broadsheet generation.

2. Subscription & Payment

  1. The Client School agrees to pay the applicable plan fee per academic term as selected in this form.
  2. A one-time setup fee of ₦10,000 is payable before or upon activation of the system.
  3. Payment must be made in full before the start of each term. Rehoteq reserves the right to restrict access for non-payment.
  4. Fees are subject to review with 30 days' notice. Existing term subscriptions are honoured at the agreed rate.
  5. Payments are non-refundable once the system has been set up and activated for the Client School.

3. Service Delivery

  1. Rehoteq Technologies will set up the Client School's RMS account within 24 hours of receiving payment and the completed onboarding form.
  2. Training and onboarding support will be provided via WhatsApp or in person where feasible.
  3. The system will be available online 24/7, subject to scheduled maintenance windows.
  4. Technical support is provided during business hours (Monday–Saturday, 8am–6pm WAT).

4. Data Privacy & Security

  1. All student data entered into the RMS is the property of the Client School.
  2. Rehoteq Technologies will not share, sell, or disclose student data to any third party without written consent from the Client School.
  3. The Client School is responsible for ensuring that all data entered is accurate and lawfully obtained.
  4. The RMS uses Firebase (Google Cloud) infrastructure with industry-standard encryption and security.

5. Client Responsibilities

  1. The Client School agrees to provide accurate school and student information during setup.
  2. The Client School will keep login credentials (passwords) confidential and not share them externally.
  3. The Client School agrees not to misuse the platform or attempt to access data belonging to other schools.
  4. The Client School will promptly notify Rehoteq of any technical issues or security concerns.

6. Intellectual Property

The Rehoteq RMS platform, including its code, design, and systems, remains the sole intellectual property of Rehoteq Technologies. The Client School is granted a non-exclusive, non-transferable licence to use the platform during the subscription period.

7. Termination

  1. Either party may terminate this agreement with 14 days' written notice.
  2. Upon termination, the Client School may request an export of their data in CSV format within 30 days.
  3. Rehoteq reserves the right to terminate immediately in cases of misuse or breach of this agreement.

8. Limitation of Liability

Rehoteq Technologies shall not be liable for any indirect, incidental, or consequential damages arising from use of the platform. The maximum liability shall not exceed the subscription fees paid in the preceding term.

9. Governing Law

This agreement is governed by the laws of the Federal Republic of Nigeria. Any disputes shall be resolved through mutual negotiation, failing which, through appropriate legal channels in Ondo State, Nigeria.

10. Entire Agreement

This document, together with the selected plan and the completed onboarding form, constitutes the entire agreement between the parties.
